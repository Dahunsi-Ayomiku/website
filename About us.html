    About us

    Nigeria is a developing country, and the need to innovate and grow is fundamental to the country's future. The Code4Kidz Club help brings dreams and ideas to reality by providing the knowledge, skill set, and conducive environment to make ideas a reality, thereby innovating and being part of the change the country needs. High unemployment has caused individuals to seek skills while employers increase their training requirements to remain competitive. Developing the country's future leaders in tech and building a generation to match global needs, decrease unemployment rates, improve employability, and enhance growth and development. The Proprietor, Dr. (Mrs) Folasade M. Dahunsi, is passionate about developing and building up the upcoming generation. After resuming her faculty position at the Federal University of Technology, Akure, after her Ph.D. studies in South Africa, she started to bridge the lack of adequate hands-on skills in current technology (such as the Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Embedded Systems, Robotics, etc.) among students. She started the Bloomtronics Tech Community in 2017 at the Federal University of Technology, Akure, geared towards training students (and interested staff) and providing an enabling environment to create innovative solutions to Africa's challenges.

    Our Aim

    Our aim is to make programming so interesting and much desired that it will be regarded as course in schools We also aim at providing our country with a generation that will raise Nigeria from the state of a developing country to a well-developed country We aim at bring back the honor, we have lost due to bad use of technology
