    Code4kids

    Code4Kidz club trains young children and teens (ages 8 – 16) in software, hardware, innovative, technology and leadership skills. The world is entering a new era beyond the information age. The club trains on current technological trends to equip our children and youth for the technological space and improve the world. We aim to bring up a generation of programmers, innovators, tech enthusiasts, designers, teachers, and entrepreneurs leading into the new era. We devote ourselves to instilling the correct values and mindset into our students, leading them to a bright future. We make our lessons fun and interactive, allowing students to grow and make their changes. We aim to demystify the ability to create and innovate, bringing ideas and dreams to reality and building generations of established, well-built technology and cutting-edge-oriented professionals to take the country and the world to the next level through innovation. Our approach ranges from coding camps to afternoon clubs and one-on-one lessons in various courses: basics of computers, web design, Python, Robotics, IoT (Internet of Things), embedded systems, artificial intelligence, and programming.
